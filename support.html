Support Imagik!

There are three ways to support the Imagik project!

Firstly, you can SHARE THE WORD. The embedded video at the home page is a FANTASTIC video to share, but also talking to friends about the project and sharing the website link around is encouraged heavily.

Secondly, you can HELP DEVELOPMENT. You can contact us at the contact page. We encourage you to use the discord listed, and not the email. Email is acceptable, however.

Last, but not least, you can DONATE.

Hosted by Robert Crenshaw, supporting his son Havoc Crenshaw.

Imagik is a project to go against Roblox and bring back the safety, community, and fun.

Imagik aims to recreate a experience similar to 2006 Roblox, modernized for today. On top of that, Imagik holds to heart strong values, putting the community first.

The Imagik project needs funding, of course. Any money helps, but we aim for around $5000 minimum as a strong base to fund servers & hosting, cover expenses regarding the development process, and any possible unforeseen costs.

We're tired of Roblox's utter disregard for the safety of the community, and their constant exploitative and predatory behavior towards children.

We thank you for participating in bringing back community and safety to the Internet, and for supporting the development of Roblox's rival platform.
