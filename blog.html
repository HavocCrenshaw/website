The Blog

What is this? This is Imagik's blog! Here, you will get the most recent info on the development cycle of Imagik. Posts are inconsistent but frequent, and share technical insights and even issues, personal things, and various other niche things that are not shared on Havoc Crenshaw's YouTube.

Entries are listed from newest (top) to oldest (bottom).

"A new look, A new horizon, A bright future" - August 24, 2025 8:03 PM

So the website got a makeover! And I also decided to destroy the old blog posts. They no longer reflect, to any degree, the direction of the platform and it would be just a big mess to explain them away so, poof!

I'm currently in the process of making the "Clippy says 'ROBLOX is a lost cause, let's make our own.'" video. The weight of having to deal with messy libraries, solo dev, the weight of not being able to perform fast enough, the works has made me come to the decision that simply I will just make a video, a call to action. Get people behind the project.

All former code has also been destroyed. We're starting fresh, and we're going to make it better than ever before. Cleaner code, stronger code, a more professional workflow, it's gonna be great, and it's gonna be greater with funding and at least one more dev behind it.

But honestly? It's also a bit anxiety inducing. I really didn't want to have to rely on the chance that people may go with the idea, so I could present it to them somewhat complete so they may be more willing to commit to it. But, alas. That did not happen.

A bit ranty. I'm just a bit uncomfortable, is all. New terrain. But I guess, any good leader, or any innovator, or any explorer, successful or unsuccessful has something like this ocassionally in their endeavours.

Ciao!
