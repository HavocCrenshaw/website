imagik™ is a currently in production, online sandbox platform where you can build your own game, and release it for friends to play!

We're not affiliated with anybody, but I think the root inspiration of what we're doing is obvious and will certainly become more obvious as time goes on.

We're a spiritual successor to what our inspirations laid down. We're bringing back the fun. The magic. The art. The community.

Get ready to Build It, Be It.
