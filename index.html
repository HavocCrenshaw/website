Welcome to Imagik!

Imagik is a solution to a problem.

Companies do not want to take responsibility for their own platform. While big businesses roll around in their monumentous amounts of money, they actively crush the consumer, and participate in borderline illegal and completely unethical practices. Case in point, recent decisions Roblox Corporation has made, especially around the safety of it's users, particularly children. It's a crazy world we live in, but it's not a world we have to live in.

What big businesses do not understand is that they do not function without their consumers. Even in a pure capitalist environment, people will give up certain luxuries to avoid supporting something they believe is hurting them. Fortunately, there is, (to my knowledge), no pure capitalist countries. Anti-monopoly laws and laws like it present in most countries actively encourage the people to take matters into their own hands.

Hence, Imagik. We won't stand for Roblox's greed. We won't stand for Roblox's continuous harassment and violation of user rights. We've had enough of it. It's time to take the power back, for the people to stand for a better digital world.

Imagik's goals are simple. We take Roblox, and we restart. Back to 2006, but brought to 2025. We'll make it modern, polished, but it serves as a fantastic base for what can become on-par with modern Roblox, but better in every way.

If you want to see this project take off, if you want to make the world a better place, if you want to make sure that Imagik can succeed so that Imagik can actually do good, to do right where Roblox and other corporations did wrong, we encourage you to support us.

I whole heartedly thank anybody who read this message, and cannot thank enough the people who choose to support the Imagik project.
